Introduction

Welcome to our female only lead site for women lifters! This page is for any aspiring women who wish to begin their powerlifting journey who don't know where to begin. This group will support and guide you from a beginner level to more advanced if you give yourself the time and patience. With our state of the art gym, ideal location and super coaches, there's nothing to lose. Let's work together! Let's get confident, let's get strong!
